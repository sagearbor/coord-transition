Transition Protocol

Peaceful local coordination during force withdrawal.

1. The Silent Vote

Place a plain white sheet on your roof or courtyard. This signals intent safely to satellites.

SIGNAL: WHITE SQUARE

2. The Town Square

At High Noon on the first Friday after bombing pauses, form a large circle in the town center.

SIGNAL: THE CIRCLE

3. Security Transition

Police/Military: If you stand with the town, form an 'X' inside the circle.

SIGNAL: CIRCLE + X
